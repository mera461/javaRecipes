ING::
1 3/4 pounds fresh green peas
1 cup peeled, cubed baking potato
1 cup chopped leek
3/4 cup water

1 cup 2% low-fat milk
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup plain low-fat yogurt

DIR::
Shell and wash peas. Combine peas and next 4 ingredients in a large sauce
