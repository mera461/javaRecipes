[Country Scalloped Potatoes]

Source: www.crock-pot.com

 

Ingredients

  • 6 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, Shredded
  • ½ cup Italian parsley, fresh, minced
  • 10 each button mushrooms, thinly sliced
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ cup butter, melted
  • ½ teaspoon paprika
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Directions

Lightly grease the stoneware. Alternate layers of potatoes, onions, cheese, parsley and mushrooms in the stoneware. In a small bowl, combine the milk, butter, paprika, pepper and salt. Pour mixture over the ingredients in slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 7 to 9 hours or on high for 3 to 4 hours.
