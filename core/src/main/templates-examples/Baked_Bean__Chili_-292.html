Baked Bean "Chili"

Ingredients

  • 2 cans pork n beans
  • 2 cans northern beans
  • 1 small can kidney beans
  • ½ pound bacon, fried
  • 1 onion
  • 1 pound hamburger
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon worchestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon mustard

Method

1. After frying bacon and hamburger, place all ingredients in crock pot.
2. Mix and heat in crock pot for desired time, at least an hour for flavors to merry.
