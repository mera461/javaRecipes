Ingredients

  • 2 envelopes Dream Whip
  • 2 ¾ cups milk, (1 1/3+ cup milk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 packages Milk Chocolate Instant Pudding, (8 oz)
  • 1 ⅓ cups more milk

Method

Beat Dream Whip, 1 1/3 cup milk
6 minutes until peaks form
Add Remaining milk, pudding, and beat for 2 minutes.
Chill for 4 hours.
